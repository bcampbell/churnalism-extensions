Churnalism extension

Works like this:

  1. User enters a url/clicks on a link/whatever
  2. extension waits for core part of page to load (can take a good few seconds, depending on the scripts in use - most newspapers are pretty bad here, with heaps of analytics crap)
  3. the article text is extracted and sent of to churnalism.com
  4. churnalism.com sends back some results.
  5. results are displayed in popup window - click on the widget in the bottom-right of the screen to activate it.
